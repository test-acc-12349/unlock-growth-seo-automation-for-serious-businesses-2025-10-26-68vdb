About NXSYS

NXSYS was founded in 2018 by a team of SEO specialists and software engineers who saw a critical gap in the market. Traditional SEO agencies were expensive, slow, and difficult to scale. Meanwhile, DIY tools were fragmented and required expertise most businesses didn't have. We set out to build a platform that would democratize access to enterprise-grade SEO automation, making it affordable and accessible for businesses of all sizes. Starting with a small team of 5 in our Sydney office, we've grown to serve over 2,000 clients across 15 countries, helping them reclaim millions of dollars in lost organic revenue.

Our mission is simple: empower businesses to compete and win in organic search without needing a team of expensive SEO consultants. We believe that intelligent automation, combined with human expertise, can transform how companies approach digital marketing. We're committed to continuous innovation, staying ahead of algorithm changes, and providing our clients with tools and insights that deliver real, measurable results. At NXSYS, we measure our success by your success—every ranking improvement, every traffic increase, and every conversion represents our shared achievement.
